Bulk SMS Gateway

Our Bulk SMS Gateway provides a high-throughput platform designed to deliver thousands of messages per second. Ideal for large-scale marketing campaigns, alerts, and informational broadcasts. We ensure maximum deliverability with direct operator connections and real-time routing optimization.

Key Features

  • Instant Delivery
  • Unicode Support
  • Real-time DLR
  • API Integration

Perfect For:

E-commerce sales, Political campaigns, Event reminders, Mass notifications.

Promotional SMS

Drive sales and engagement with targeted Promotional SMS. This service allows you to reach a wide audience with marketing offers, discounts, and brand updates. We use smart filtering to target active users while maintaining compliance with TRAI regulations (DND scrubbing).

Key Features

  • Sender ID
  • DND Filter
  • High Open Rates
  • Analytics Dashboard

Perfect For:

Flash sales, Seasonal offers, New product launches, Loyalty programs.

Transactional SMS

Critical information demands 100% reliability. Our Transactional SMS service delivers OTPs, order confirmations, and bank alerts via priority routes with 99.99% uptime. These messages reach users regardless of their DND status, ensuring vital updates are never missed.

Key Features

  • Priority Routing
  • 24/7 Uptime
  • DND Bypass
  • Instant Push

Perfect For:

2FA/OTPs, Banking alerts, Order tracking, Appointment reminders.

WhatsApp Promotional

Leverage the world's most popular messaging app for marketing. Send rich media messages including images, videos, and PDFs directly to your customers' WhatsApp. Benefit from higher engagement rates compared to traditional SMS marketing.

Key Features

  • Rich Media
  • Template Messages
  • High Engagement
  • Call-to-Action

Perfect For:

Product catalogs, Festival greetings, Bill payment reminders, Abandoned cart recovery.

WhatsApp Business API

Integrate WhatsApp directly into your CRM and support workflows. The official API allows multiple agents to manage conversations, automate responses with chatbots, and provide seamless customer support at scale.

Key Features

  • Multi-Agent Access
  • Chatbot Integration
  • Official Provider
  • CRM Sync

Perfect For:

Customer support desks, Booking confirmations, Automated lead generation.

RCS Messaging

Rich Communication Services (RCS) is the evolution of SMS. Send branded messages with high-resolution images, carousels, and suggested replies directly within the native messaging app. No app download required for the user.

Key Features

  • Rich Cards
  • Suggested Actions
  • Brand Verification
  • Fallback to SMS

Perfect For:

Airline boarding passes, Retail browsing, Rich receipts.

Voice Solutions

Key Features

  • Voice Broadcasting
  • IVR Systems
  • Click-to-Call
  • Number Masking

Perfect For:

Lead verification, Political campaigns, Customer surveys, Privacy calls (Uber-style).
